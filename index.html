Experiences

Center for Instructional Technology

At CIT I was tasked with the construction of interactive learning objects. Learning objects are animated projects that are aimed to promote online learning. These objects are particularly useful to teachers with online classes, since they enhance the learning experience for the students. Most of my projects were built in Adobe Edge Animate. I utilized the tools and libaries available (HTML, JavaScript, and JQuery) to construct my most interesting ideas.
