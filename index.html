Experiences

Center for Instructional Technology (CIT)

At CIT (department under Westfield State University that supports and train faculty with online classes), I was tasked with the construction of interactive learning objects. Learning objects are animated projects with an aim to promote online learning.

Aside from learning new tools(Edge Animate, Dream Weaver) and languages(HTML, JavaScript, JQuery), I also got the opportunity to take on new challenges outside of my comfort zone. With every hour I spent on each project, an equivalent hour is used to develop my own application that encompasses my collective understanding of my work.
